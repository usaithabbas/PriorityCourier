About Priority Courier

At Priority Courier Service, we take immense pride in our legacy of 36 years as a trusted courier company in Chennai and beyond. Our journey began with a commitment to excellence, a commitment that has only grown stronger over time.

Keerthana Courier Service has consistently evolved to meet the changing needs of our valued customers. What started as a local courier service in Chennai has expanded into a comprehensive network, providing reliable, nationwide, and international courier solutions.

Our Commitment


Our commitment to excellence is unwavering. We understand that each shipment is more than just a package; it's a promise. A promise of timely delivery, security, and peace of mind for our customers. Over the years, we have built a reputation for reliability, transparency, and affordability.

To be the first choice for individuals and businesses in need of reliable, secure, and efficient courier services. We aim to continue expanding our reach, offering nationwide and international courier solutions while upholding our core values of transparency and affordability.
